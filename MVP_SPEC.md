# 7anoutiAI MVP

Goal:
Help Moroccan small shop owners track stock, sales, and restocking needs.

Target users:
Small hanout/grocery owners in Morocco.

Main features:
1. Add, edit, and delete products.
2. Record sales.
3. Automatically update stock after each sale.
4. Show dashboard:
   - Today’s revenue
   - Today’s profit
   - Low-stock products
   - Best-selling products
5. Give simple restocking recommendations based on stock and recent sales.

Tech stack:
Python, Streamlit, SQLite, Pandas.

Not included in MVP:
- No payment system.
- No user accounts.
- No mobile app yet.
- No barcode scanner yet.
- No advanced AI chatbot yet.
- No Darija voice input yet.

Future improvements:
- Darija voice input.
- WhatsApp integration.
- Barcode scanning.
- Cloud sync.
- Real AI chatbot.
